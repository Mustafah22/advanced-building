F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and how we work.

01

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We operate with the required licenses and carry liability insurance. Documentation is available on request.

02

Do you offer emergency call‑outs?

We accommodate urgent requests whenever possible. Mention “urgent” in your message and we’ll prioritize your ticket.

03

How do you price jobs?

After a quick call and (often free) site visit, you’ll receive a written quote with scope, materials, and a clear timeline.

04

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the local region. If you’re nearby, reach out— we’ll confirm availability for your location.

05

We’re a new company—can you provide references?

As we begin operations, we’re happy to offer trial jobs, transparent reporting, and warranties. References will be added as projects complete.
